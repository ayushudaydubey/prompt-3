Satyarthi Foundation

The Satyarthi Foundation, led by Nobel Peace Laureate Kailash Satyarthi, is committed to ensuring freedom, education, and safety for every child. Founded in 2004, the foundation actively works to eradicate child labor, human trafficking, and all forms of exploitation. With its mission to create a world where children are free to enjoy their childhood, the organization has rescued and rehabilitated thousands of children while advocating for their rights.
